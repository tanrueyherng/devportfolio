Projects

YCEP24

From the previous experience, I got to know about the YCEP programme which is a 3-day programme for secondary school students to get us excited about a cybersecurity career. Through this course, I learnt more about Cybersecurity such as how to use web tools such as Cyberchef to decode encrypted texts, Wireshark to monitor network traffic, Image Steganography etc. On the third day of the YCEP programme, a capture the flag competition was organised to test the skills we have learnt so far. The challenges ranged from osint, pwn, web, forensics, crypto. This would be my second capture the flag competition and I felt more confident than the last as I learnt a lot from the first two days of the programme. I was surprised that I managed to solve 25 challenges and come in 7th, tying with the 6th person.
